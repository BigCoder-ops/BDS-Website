Who We Are
Sport is not
a privilege.

The BDS is the official student-led sports body of UM6P Rabat. We organize, manage and elevate every sports experience on campus — from casual games to competitive tournaments. Every student has a place to play, compete and belong.

01
Action-Ready
Full 12-month plan built before day one. No guesswork — pure execution from the start.
02
Truly Inclusive
3 nationalities, gender-balanced leadership — a team as diverse as the students we represent.
03
Student-Built
Built around real student feedback. More consistent events, better communication, diverse sports.
04
Transparent
Every budget, every event, every outcome — documented and shared openly with the campus.

Collaboration

Sport and culture go hand in hand. The BDS and BDE are stronger together — combining athletic energy with cultural creativity to build the ultimate campus experience.

BDS
Bureau Des Sports
We bring the athletic energy — tournaments, competitions, fitness events and sports culture across UM6P Rabat.
  • Sports tournaments & competitions
  • Fitness & wellness programs
  • Inter-faculty sports leagues
  • Campus running events
  • Sports equipment & facilities
BDE

They bring the cultural heartbeat — events, entertainment, social activities and student life across campus.
  • Campus social events & galas
  • Cultural celebrations
  • Entertainment & music
  • Student networking
  • Communications & visibility
